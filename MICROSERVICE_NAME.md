# Microservice Name

## Management Reporting

**Service Name:** Management Reporting  
**Service Identifier:** `ManagementReporting`  
**Package Name:** `management-reporting-backend`  
**Full Name:** EducoreAI Management Reporting Microservice

---

## Service Details

- **Service Type:** Backend Microservice
- **Purpose:** Centralized reporting and analytics platform for EducoreAI ecosystem
- **Main Functionality:** Aggregates data from multiple microservices and provides unified dashboards, reports, and analytics

---

## Related Services

This microservice integrates with the following EducoreAI microservices:

1. **Directory** - User and organization management
2. **Course Builder** - Course creation and enrollment tracking
3. **Assessment** - Skill evaluations and test results
4. **Content Studio** - Content creation metrics
5. **Learning Analytics** - Aggregated learning performance statistics
6. **RAG** - AI report conclusions service

---

## Service Endpoints

- **Base URL:** `/api`
- **Dashboard:** `/api/v1/dashboard`
- **Reports:** `/api/v1/reports`
- **RAG Endpoint:** `/api/fill-content-metrics`
